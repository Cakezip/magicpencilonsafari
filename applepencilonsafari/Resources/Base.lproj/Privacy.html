Policy

Privacy Policy

Magic Pencil is built to work without user accounts and without sending browsing annotations to the developer.

Data Collection

Magic Pencil does not intentionally collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or with third parties for advertising or analytics.

On-Device Behavior

The extension stores lightweight preferences such as whether the overlay is enabled, which ink color is selected, and the preferred fade duration in local browser storage on the device.

Annotations

Temporary strokes are rendered on the current web page and fade automatically. They are not synced to a server and are not designed to persist across devices.

Third Parties

The app does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or external sign-in providers.

Retention

Visible strokes are intentionally short-lived. Local settings remain on the device until the app or browser data is removed.

Your Choices

You can disable the extension in Safari settings, clear the current page from the extension popup, and remove the app at any time.

Publisher Information

A public copy of this policy should also be available from the app’s App Store product page.
