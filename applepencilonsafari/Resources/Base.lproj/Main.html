Safari Extension Companion

Magic Pencil

Temporary Apple Pencil notes for Safari on iPad. Pencil draws. Fingers keep scrolling.

What It Does

Magic Pencil overlays temporary or session-long strokes on top of Safari web pages. Notes stay attached to the page while you scroll and can either fade away automatically or remain until the page session ends.

  • Apple Pencil for drawing
  • Finger gestures for scrolling
  • Volatile or session-long ink modes
  • 5s, 10s, 15s, 30s, or 45s hold times
  • Black or white ink

Turn It On

  1. Open Settings on iPad.
  2. Go to Apps > Safari > Extensions.
  3. Enable Magic Pencil.
  4. Allow it on the websites where you want to write.
  5. Return to Safari and reload the page once.

How To Use It

Draw

Touch the page with Apple Pencil and write directly on the article, search results, or any other normal web page.

Control

Open the extension popup in Safari to switch between volatile and session modes, pause the overlay, clear the page, switch ink color, or adjust fade duration.

Privacy

Magic Pencil is designed to stay on-device. It does not require sign in, accounts, analytics, advertising, or cloud sync for its core functionality.
