## Promotional Text

Write temporary or session-long notes directly on Safari pages with Apple Pencil. Strokes move with the page, support quick fade timing or session hold, and leave finger scrolling untouched.

## Description

Magic Pencil adds a lightweight Safari overlay for fast, temporary Pencil notes on iPad.

Use Apple Pencil to sketch, underline, or mark up ideas while reading the web. Your notes stay attached to the page as you scroll, then disappear automatically after a short delay.

Core features:
- Apple Pencil drawing on Safari pages
- Finger scrolling stays available
- Volatile mode with automatic fade
- Session mode that lasts until the page reloads, navigates, or Safari closes
- 5s, 10s, 15s, 30s, and 45s hold times
- Black or white ink
- Per-page clear action

Magic Pencil is designed for quick thinking, not clutter. It helps you react to what you are reading without turning every page into a permanent canvas.

Privacy by design:
- No account required
- No ads
- No analytics SDKs
- No cloud sync for notes
