Official support hub for Magic Pencil

Draw on Safari with Apple Pencil, while fingers keep scrolling.

Magic Pencil is an iPad Safari extension for fast, lightweight web annotation. Ink can fade away after a few seconds, last for the whole browsing session, or stay until the tab is refreshed.

Open Support Privacy Policy

What It Does

Apple Pencil draws. Finger touches are left free for normal page scrolling.

Volatile mode fades quickly. Choose short-lived ink for thought sketches that disappear on purpose.

Session mode persists. Keep notes on the current page until refresh, navigation, or leaving Safari.

Simple controls. Toggle black or white ink, change hold time, or clear the page from the Safari extension popup.
