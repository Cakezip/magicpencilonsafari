Magic Pencil Privacy Policy

Magic Pencil is designed to work without accounts and without transmitting browsing annotations to the developer.

Data Collection

Magic Pencil does not intentionally collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties for analytics or advertising.

On-Device Preferences

The app stores lightweight preferences such as fade duration, enabled state, and ink color in local browser storage on the device.

Annotations

Temporary strokes are rendered locally on the current Safari page and fade automatically. They are not synced to a server.

Third Parties

The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party sign-in providers.

Retention

Visible strokes are intentionally short-lived. Local settings remain on the device until the app or browser data is removed.

Your Choices

You can disable the extension in Safari settings, clear the current page from the extension popup, and remove the app at any time.
